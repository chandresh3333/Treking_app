1. Project Details
Project Title
Trekking Management Application
Problem Statement
Adventure organizations often manage trekking activities through spreadsheets, phone calls, and manual coordination. These traditional approaches create several challenges:
Difficulty managing trek registrations
Overbooking of trekking slots
Lack of proper staff assignment
No centralized booking history
Limited visibility of trek status
Time-consuming administrative work
The objective of this project is to develop a web-based Trekking Management Application that enables efficient management of trekking activities through role-based access for Admin, Trek Staff, and Users.
2. Approach to the Problem Statement
The application was developed using the Flask framework with SQLite as the backend database.
The system follows a role-based architecture where:
Admin
Manages treks
Approves staff
Assigns staff to treks
Blacklists users/staff
Monitors bookings and statistics
Trek Staff
Registers and waits for approval
Views assigned treks
Updates trek status and slots
Manages participant lists
Users
Register and login
Search treks
Book treks
View booking history
Update profile information
The application ensures:
Prevention of overbooking
Staff access restrictions
Booking only for open treks
Historical tracking of trekking activities
